Alexandria Rose - known as Lexi - is 15 going on 25. She is a fun-loving teenager who values family more than most kids her age. She is an amazing big sister, a wonderful friend, and a fabulous daughter...except when she's not.

Isabella Jolie is like 50 pounds of dynomite in a 3 pound package. She is kind and sweet when she chooses to be, but under the sweet face and pretty blue eyes lies a serious force to be reckoned with. Her leadership abilities are phenomenal. She idolizes her big brother and loves taking care of her little sister.

Annika Kyriel is a cuddle bug with a stubborn streak. Usually very sweet, attentive, and kind, she occasionally turns into a mini tyrant with a good sense of humor. She is spoiled rotton, and positive she's going to be a princess when she grows up. She loves singing and dancing, and playing outside with her brother.
